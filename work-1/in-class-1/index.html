About Me

Andrew Kunselman attended Central Valley High School where he was born and raised in Aliquippa Pennsylvania. He graduated in 2015 and enrolled in the University of Pittsburgh where he is a current Junior majoring in Marketing and Business Information Systems with a Certificate in Digital Media. Andrew has also received his Google AdWords Certification in his spare time and has run two successful political online advertising campaigns. One was a state-wide race in Pennsylvania, and one was in Beaver County. Andrew’s passion for sports and incredible networking capabilities have landed him a job as an Equipment Manager for the University of Pittsburgh Football team where he distributes and routinely fixes helmets, shoulder pads, and other equipment. As an Equipment Manager he is also responsible for pre-game and post-game locker room set up and clean up, including traveling to away games, and providing laundry service for members of the team and staff.
